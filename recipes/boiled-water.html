♨️ Boiled Water

The perfect recipe for any new cook. Professional results in just seven minutes. "And it's gluten free, too!"

Ingredients

  • Water.

Instructions

  1. Pour desired amount of water into a heat-safe pot or kettle.
  2. Place the pot/kettle on a stovetop burner or electric kettle base.
  3. Turn on the heat source to a high setting.
  4. Observe the water as it begins to form small bubbles, then larger bubbles, leading to a vigorous rolling boil.
  5. Once the water reaches a full, rolling boil, carefully turn off the heat source.
  6. Allow to cool slightly before handling, or use immediately for your desired purpose (e.g., tea, cooking).
