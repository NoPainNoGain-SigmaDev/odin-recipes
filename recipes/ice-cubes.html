"Mix 1 cup of water with 1 tablespoon of water, then add more water to enchance taste."

Ingredients

  • Water.

Instructions

  1. Fill an ice cube tray with water. Pour carefully to avoid spills.
  2. Place the filled tray into a freezer. Ensure it's on a level surface so the water doesn't slosh out.
  3. Wait a few hours (typically 2-4 hours, depending on your freezer's temperature) until the water has completely frozen solid.
  4. Remove the tray from the freezer.
  5. Twist the tray or push on the bottom of the individual cubes to release your freshly made ice.
  6. Add to your favorite beverage and enjoy!
